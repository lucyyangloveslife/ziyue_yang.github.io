My Story

I'm Ziyue Yang, currently pursuing an MSc in Financial Technology at University College London, following my First-Class BSc in Financial Mathematics from the University of Liverpool and Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University. During my undergraduate studies in Financial Mathematics, I systematically acquired core knowledge in both finance and mathematics, with a particular fascination for statistics. Initially, I was deeply immersed in the world of model derivation and theoretical proofs, drawn to the rigor and elegance of mathematical logic. However, as I delved deeper into my studies, I gradually realized that what truly excited me was the process of translating abstract mathematical concepts into executable code and applying them to solve complex, real-world problems. Driven by a passion for the integration of data and technology, I joined the MSc Financial Technology program at UCL. Here, I gained in-depth knowledge of machine learning, blockchain, and other technologies. Through various projects and assignments, I was able to turn theoretical knowledge into practical, implementable solutions, which honed my ability to think independently and work hands-on. I aspire to become a data scientist who combines data and algorithms to tell compelling, real-world stories through visualization and modeling.
